How to read this page

PAIN ##
Pain signal. Customers complaining in reviews about dispatch failures, missed callbacks, or abandoned jobs. Number shows the pain dimension score (0-40).
GROWTH ##
Growth signal. Hiring postings, review velocity acceleration, or momentum language in reviews. Number shows the growth dimension score (0-25).
DEMAND PULL ##
Demand pull. One-person shop winning on reputation. Customers switching from competitors, owner named in reviews, positive review bursts. Number shows the demand-pull score (0-20).
NO FSM
No field service platform. Website fingerprinting detected no ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, or online booking widget. Bookings happen by phone or web form.
HIRING DISPATCH
Actively hiring a dispatcher. Open job posting titled Dispatcher, Scheduling Coordinator, or CSR. They are hiring the role FSM software directly replaces.
THIN SAMPLE (#)
Thin sample warning. LLM review analysis ran on fewer than 10 cached reviews. Signal scores derived from review text are discounted proportionally.
